# Code of Conduct

We want this project to be a welcoming space for contributors of all
backgrounds and experience levels.

## Our Pledge

We pledge to make participation a respectful and constructive experience for
everyone, regardless of age, body size, disability, ethnicity, gender identity
and expression, level of experience, nationality, personal appearance, race,
religion, or sexual identity and orientation.

## Expected Behavior

- Be kind, patient, and considerate.
- Assume good intent. Ask clarifying questions before reacting strongly.
- Give and accept constructive feedback gracefully.
- Focus on what is best for the project and the wider community.
- Acknowledge mistakes and learn from them.

## Unacceptable Behavior

- Harassment, intimidation, or discrimination of any kind.
- Trolling, insulting comments, or personal attacks.
- Public or private harassment of contributors.
- Publishing others' private information without explicit permission.
- Sustained disruption of discussions, issues, or pull requests.

## Scope

This Code of Conduct applies to all project spaces — issues, pull requests,
discussions, code review comments — and to public spaces when an individual
is representing the project.

## Reporting

Report concerns privately to **sean.katz@gmail.com** with subject prefix
`[ninja-translate conduct]`. Reports are handled confidentially. The
maintainer will review and respond within 7 days.

## Enforcement

The maintainer may warn, temporarily ban, or permanently remove contributors
whose behavior violates this Code. The decision is final, but contributors
may appeal once in writing to the same email.

This Code of Conduct is adapted from the
[Contributor Covenant](https://www.contributor-covenant.org/), version 2.1.
